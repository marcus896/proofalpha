# Community Code of Conduct

ProofAlpha contributors must communicate respectfully and focus criticism on ideas, code, evidence, and documented behavior.

Expected conduct:

- welcome contributors of different experience levels;
- explain decisions and evidence clearly;
- respect privacy and security boundaries;
- avoid harassment, discrimination, threats, personal attacks, and unwanted contact;
- do not promote guaranteed returns, deceptive performance claims, or financial pressure;
- disclose conflicts of interest when relevant.

Maintainers may edit, hide, reject, or remove contributions and participation that violate these expectations. Serious or repeated violations may result in temporary or permanent exclusion from project spaces.

Report conduct concerns privately to the project contact listed in `MAINTAINERS.md` once configured. Do not disclose another person's private information in public reports.
